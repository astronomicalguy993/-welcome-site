Paragliding in Spain: A Ukrainian's Joy

For many Ukrainians living or visiting Spain, paragliding offers not just an adventure, but a deep breath of freedom and beauty. The vast Spanish skies, gentle winds, and breathtaking views from above give a sense of lightness that helps leave worries behind.

It's more than a sport — it's a reminder that peace and wonder still exist. Flying over the hills and coastlines, many feel closer to home, to nature, and to themselves.

Meet Stas Hordiienko – Licensed Paragliding Instructor

Stas Hordiienko is a licensed paragliding pilot with years of experience in the skies. Over the years, he has flown across Ukraine, Hungary, and the Czech Republic, building a deep understanding of safe and thrilling paragliding adventures.

Now, Stas brings his passion to Spain — offering unforgettable flights in the stunning region of Alicante, part of the Valencian Community. Whether you're a beginner or an enthusiast, you now have the opportunity to soar with an experienced and certified guide.
